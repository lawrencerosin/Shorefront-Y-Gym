About Shorefront Y Gym

Shorefront Y has a gym where people can exercise and enjoy it.
Shorefront Y has balls for many different sports, and people can
can use those ball to play those sports. But this place isn't
limited to ball sports. We also can do other exercises such as
spinning hula hoops. And if you don't know how to do a certain
exercise or need help with it, Shorefront Y has coaches that can
help you learn it or improve on it.
